Basic Attacks

You must be in the same crew as your stand for these to work. Use Crew! / Uncrew! if you have a valid group.
Attack!Toggles attack mode.
Unattack!Stops attacking.
Stab!Performs a stab attack.
Unstab!Stops stabbing (same as Unattack!).
gkill!Kills the target. Use Unattack! or re-summon to stop.

Attack Modes

Combat!Default combat attack.
Knife!Knife attack.
Pitch!Pitchfork attack with a bigger hitbox.
Sign!Stop sign attack with a bigger hitbox.
Whip!Whip attack with a bigger hitbox.
Hidden!Stand attacks from under the target.
Default!Stand attacks the target from the sky.
Drop!Drops the current target.
Throw!Throws the current target.
Barrage! / Muda! / Ora!Attack rushes.

Gun Stuff

Gun!Stand buys a gun and ammo.
.rifleStand uses rifle.
.lmgStand uses LMG.
.augStand uses AUG.
Usage Example: .Knock [plr] or .Knock Ticxyy. Works on display names and usernames.
.Bring [user]Brings target to you.
.Smite [user]Flings the target.
.View [user]Views the target.
.Frame [user]Makes the stand follow the target.
.Bag [user]Bags the player.
.Arrest [user]Arrests the target (stand must be police).
.Knock [user]Knocks the target (alias: .k).
.Pull [user]Snatches hair from the target.
.Taser [user]Tasers the target.
.Autokill [user]Loop kills the target.
.Stomp [user]Stomps the target.
.Annoy [user]Annoys the target.
.Kannoy [user]Annoys the target with a knife.
.Gknock [user]Knocks the target with a gun.

.Gauto [user]Autokills the target with a gun.
.Fstomp [user]Stomps the target with flame.
.Fknock [user]Knocks the target with flame.
.Rk [user]Breaks the target's right leg.
.Rm [user]Destroys the target's body.
